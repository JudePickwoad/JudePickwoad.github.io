Belize

I'm from Belize, a country located in the Caribbean. English is the official language of Belize, and most of the population are multilingual.

Electrical Engineer

Throughout my Electrical Engineering degree at Memorial University, I specialized in the Power stream, focusing on courses such as Electromechanical Devices, Rotating Machines, Power Electronics, and Renewable Energy Systems. My coursework provided a strong foundation in electrical power generation, transmission, and control, complemented by hands-on experience in industrial controls and supervisory data acquisition. Additionally, my capstone Electrical Engineering Design Project allowed me to apply my knowledge to real-world power applications, reinforcing my technical and analytical skills.

Swimming Instructor

While at YMCA Belize, I embraced the challenge of becoming a swimming instructor despite not being a strong swimmer. My dedication and eagerness to learn new skills drove me to apply my training quickly and effectively. I went above and beyond to support my students, even earning heartfelt praise from a parent for my extra effort in helping a struggling learner. Committed to delivering the best instruction possible, I took the initiative to research innovative teaching techniques, ensuring that every student received the guidance they needed to succeed. This experience strengthened my adaptability and perseverance while reinforcing my passion for mentorship and skill development.

B.I.O.N.I.C Club Member

At Saint John’s Junior College, I was an active member of B.I.O.N.I.C.S. (Believe It Or Not I Care), a mental health club dedicated to supporting student well-being and fostering a compassionate community. Collaborating with the school counsellor, I participated in mental health workshops that equipped me with valuable skills in empathy and advocacy. Beyond campus, our club engaged in impactful community outreach, from repairing special education facilities to delivering hampers to impoverished families. My involvement in B.I.O.N.I.C.S. reinforced my deep commitment to community building and improving the well-being of others, driving me to take meaningful action to create positive change.

Hiking and Exploring

In my free time, I immerse myself in nature, finding inspiration and peace outdoors. Since moving to St. John’s, I have explored Newfoundland’s breathtaking landscapes, from the rugged cliffs of Bonavista to the serene trails of La Manche, Beaches Trail, and the iconic Signal Hill. Each journey deepens my appreciation for the province’s untamed beauty and fuels my sense of adventure. Whether it’s hiking along coastal trails, discovering hidden coves, or simply enjoying the tranquillity of nature, I am always eager to explore new places. My love for the outdoors keeps me active and strengthens my connection to the environment, reinforcing my passion for sustainability and eco-conscious design.
